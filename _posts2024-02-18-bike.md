This is a Belarusian Minsk. A very old one. Back in the day in VN, when you worked for the Man or kinda rich, you were able to afford one of these.
 
This bike is the living and breathing (fire) example of Soviet approach to engineering. Well thought out. Very easy to work on. Rough and tough, always work even with minimal maintenance ( aka the Ay Kay 47 ). Looks good, sounds good, smells good too. Also, can hauls 2 pigs on 2 sides and still run okay.
